End-to-end flow

From a campaign brief to a tracked, multilingual send

Every campaign moves through the same eight-step pipeline — the first four are live today, the rest are next on the roadmap.

01

Campaign creation Live

Define the campaign type — awareness drive, emergency alert, educational notice, or announcement.

02

Audience selection & segmentation Live

Filter recipients by geography, language, department, occupation, and tags — with a live match count.

03

AI content generation Live

Draft the message from a brief — aware of tone, channel, and audience context.

04

Multilingual translation & personalization Live

Tone-preserving translation plus recipient-level personalization tokens.

05

Communication channel selection Next

Route each campaign to email, SMS, WhatsApp, push, or web broadcast.

06

Automated message distribution Next

Scheduled, automated delivery with retry handling.

07

Engagement tracking & feedback Next

Open, click, and response tracking per recipient.

08

Analytics & performance dashboard Next

Campaign reach and effectiveness, broken down by language and region.
